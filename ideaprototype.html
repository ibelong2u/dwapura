Hardware Product

Design & Engineering of Prototype

Typical Cost: Professional Patent Search and Attorney Opinion, $3000

Our team of expert designers and engineers will assess your product idea on three levels throughout the process: Desired function, Aesthetics, Cost & Ease of Manufacturing. You will receive ongoing feedback as we move through the prototyping process as valuable information is learned along the way. At the end of the process, you will receive a report of this analysis along with recommended steps forward.

3D illustration of Design

Typical Cost: Professional Patent Search and Attorney Opinion, $3000

In preparation for building your prototype our design team will create a 3D illustration or CAD file of your product along with schematic images. Several iterations will be made as adjustments are made to the design. You will receive copies of all illustrations and schematic drawings.

Three 3D Prints of design

Typical Cost: Professional Patent Search and Attorney Opinion, $3000

For most hardware products, we will use a 3D printer to develop the prototype. With each print, analysis will be conducted to enhance the design for functionality, aesthetics, and manufacturing cost. You will receive a report of this analysis of next steps
