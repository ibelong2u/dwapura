Leadership Team

Phillip Mertz - CEO

Phillip is a serial entrepreneur and has founded multiple companies including Secure Open Solutions, a cybersecurity firm that provides compliance services to defense technology companies and Py Square a software development company focused on developing practical software solutions for the legal industry. He is also a board member of Alpha Cognition, a biopharmaceutical company developing therapies for the prevention of neurodegenerative diseases. Phillip is a partner in the investment entity Mertz Holdings and is also the co-founder of D&M Labs, a neuroscience research institute based in Great Falls, Virginia. He graduated from Harvard University in 2006 with a A.B. in Economics.

Aditya Shrivastava - CTO

Aditya has assisted numerous companies with their technical needs including large corporations like Citibank, Microsoft and Dominos as well as numerous startups and small businesses. He was previously the director of Digirise Media Interactive and has managed teams in all aspects of software development and coding. In addition to his work in the private sector Aditya has contributed to the digital operations of multiple non-profit, public university and government organizations in the US, UAE, and India. He graduated from Texas A&M University with a B.S. in Computer Engineering in 2006 and a M.S. in Computer Science 2008.
